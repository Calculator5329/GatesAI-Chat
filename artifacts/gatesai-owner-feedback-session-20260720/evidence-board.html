How to record feedback

This board does not submit anything.

Choose a trust score and add notes. “Build evidence review” formats the current board into plain text for manual copy into chat, Forge, or Visions. Refresh clears the board.

Review the review

  1. Speed: time to first useful reaction.
  2. Clarity: obvious claim and question.
  3. Trust: honest evidence and uncertainty.
  4. Taste: room for nuanced preference.
  5. Effort: energy needed to finish.

Score each 1–5, then say whether this board should be the default.
